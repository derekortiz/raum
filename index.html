Meet Raum

From a spatially challenged apartment in Brooklyn Klaus Mueller and Christina Schneider sought to revolutionize the way we look at space.

They brought smart modular furniture to the forefront of urban living. Their furniture’s modern design compliments any cramped space. When your furniture limits your space get Raum furniture to your fitting.
